Paul Kitsaros

Paul is a bespoke tailor based in Cleveland Street in Fitzrovia, central London.
Paul initially started his career at the age of fifteen working as a tailor apprentice for several of the most prestigious tailors in Saville Row.
Passion, dedication and over 50 years of work with the best masters has provided Paul with craftsmanship of an accomplished tailor.
Personalised work and customer satisfaction are the basis of his work.
Made to measure suits and shirts are available for Ladies and Gentleman at competitive prices.
